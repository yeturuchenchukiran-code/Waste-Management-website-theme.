🏨 Hospitality · Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru

From 100% Landfill to Zero Waste in 18 Months

Oberoi Hotels generated over 12 tonnes of mixed waste daily across their Indian portfolio — nearly all of which went directly to landfill. Regulatory pressure and ESG reporting requirements made the status quo untenable.

⚡ The Challenge

Luxury properties generating 12 T/day of mixed waste across 3 cities, with no segregation infrastructure and a vendor ecosystem resistant to change.

✅ Stackly's Solution

Deployed on-site bio-composting units, kitchen biogas digesters, and a food rescue program that redirected 340 kg/day of surplus food to NGOs — all managed on Stackly OS in real time.
